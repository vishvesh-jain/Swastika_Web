Incepted in the year 1996, Swastika Components Pvt. Ltd. is engaged in the manufacturing
and exporting wide range of sintered bushes and parts for a diverse range of industries like
automobile, home appliances and many others. An ISO 9001: 2015 certified firm, we are
one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of bushes, bronze bushes, bearing bushes,
metal bushes, industrial bushes, etc. with more than 1000 types.


Our commitment towards excellence has made us to deliver optimum quality spares that are
designed and developed after a thorough analysis of the industry requirement. We conduct
rigorous test at each stage of manufacturing to ensure that the end product is in line with the
client expectation.

Application Areas


Offering an extensive range of sintered parts such as auto components, auto spare parts, selflubricated bushes, auto bearings, sintered bushes and other such parts, we cater to a large
number of clients in varied industries that are:


• Automobile Industry
• Home Appliances, Fans & coolers
• Office Equipment & Printers
• Electric and electronic industry
• Locks & hardware
• Textile machinery
• Jute Industry
• Hand tools & grinders
• Other Industrial requirements
• Overseas requirements

Products We Offer


The knowledge of our engineers, use of sophisticated machines and processes have helped us design and develop a vast range of spare parts and allow us to cater to multiple industries
effectively and efficiently.
We manufacture products like


Sintered Bushes
• Self-Lubricating bushes
• Powder Metal parts
• Sintered iron
• Sintered bronze bushes
• Plain bushes
• Flanged bushes
• Spherical bushes
• Half spherical bushes

• Washers
• Sintered gears
• Sintered rotors
• Sintered alloy steel parts

We also manufacture customized bushes as per the client’s requirements.
