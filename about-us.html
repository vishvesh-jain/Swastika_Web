About Us

We “Swastika Components Private Limited“ established in the year 1996, are involved in Manufacturing and Trading a range of Bronze Bushes, Sintered Bushes, Cam Locks, Machine Components, etc. Our dedicated approach to business has helped us to cater to clients in the best possible manner. For attaining the utmost client approval and satisfaction, we are involved in implementing the professional and client-centric approach. We are able to ensure that all the products dispatched from our end are faultless and defect-free. Inspected by professional analysts of quality, our products guarantee long life. We have installed hi-tech machinery and tools in our production section that enables us to manufacture world-class products. Keeping in mind the convenience of our customers, we deliver the offered range of products within the assured time frame at the customers’ end.

The motivation offered by our Proprietor, “Mr. K K Sadani”, inspires our workforce to give their best to the business and never compromise on the qualitative aspect of our products.
